    Privacy Policy

    Effective date: 1 January 2026. Last updated: 1 January 2026.

    01. Who We Are

    This website is operated by Markmill Trade (Company ID to be added), with its registered office at Kaprova 42/14, Stare Mesto, 110 00 Praha 1.

    We provide digital marketing, advertising, campaign strategy, performance marketing, creative systems, marketing operations, conversion intelligence, and related business support services to companies across Europe and internationally.

    For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR") and applicable data protection laws, Markmill Trade acts as the data controller in respect of personal data collected through this website.

    We are committed to protecting the personal data of our website visitors, prospective clients, clients, and business partners. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

    02. Data We Collect

    We may collect personal data when you interact with our website, submit a form, communicate with us, or engage our services.

    The types of data we may collect include:

    • Identity data — name, surname, company name, and job title.
    • Contact data — email address, phone number, and business address.
    • Financial data — invoicing details, tax or VAT numbers, and billing-related information, where applicable.
    • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, time zone, and system data.
    • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, navigation paths, and interactions.
    • Marketing data — preferences for receiving communications and campaign-related interactions.
    • Business data — information shared in relation to services, such as marketing goals, budgets, project scope, campaign requirements, and performance context.

    We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data. If such data is provided to us, it will be deleted where appropriate.

    03. How We Use Your Data

    We may use personal data to respond to inquiries and contact requests, provide and improve our services, manage client relationships and communications, process invoices and business transactions, analyse website performance and user behaviour, support marketing and advertising activities, ensure website security and prevent misuse, and comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

    We do not use your data for unrelated purposes without informing you.

    04. Legal Basis for Processing

    We rely on the following legal bases:

    • Contract performance — when processing is necessary to provide services or take steps before entering into a contract.
    • Legitimate interests — to improve services, ensure security, communicate with business contacts, and operate our business.
    • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws and regulations.
    • Consent — where required, such as for certain marketing communications or cookies.

    You may withdraw consent at any time.

    05. Data Retention

    We retain data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

    • Client and financial data — up to 7 years, where required for accounting, tax, or legal recordkeeping.
    • Inquiry data — up to 2 years after the last communication.
    • Analytics data — up to 26 months, unless a shorter period is configured.
    • Marketing data — until consent is withdrawn or the data is no longer needed.

    We may anonymise data for analytical and business improvement purposes.

    06. Sharing Your Data

    We do not sell your personal data.

    We may share data with service providers, such as hosting, CRM, analytics, email, security, and website operation tools; advertising platforms, such as Google or Meta, where applicable; professional advisers, such as legal, accounting, or tax advisers; and authorities where required by law.

    All third parties are expected to handle data securely and lawfully.

    07. International Transfers

    Some providers, including analytics, advertising, hosting, or communication platforms, may process data outside the European Economic Area.

    Where this occurs, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions, or other approved data transfer mechanisms.

    08. Your Rights

    Subject to applicable law, you have the right to access your data, correct inaccurate data, request deletion, restrict processing, receive your data in a portable format, object to processing, and withdraw consent.

    To exercise your rights, contact us using the details below.

    You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Czech Data Protection Authority (UOOU) or another competent supervisory authority.

    09. Cookies

    We use cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality, analyse usage, and support marketing efforts where applicable.

    For full details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

    10. Contact Us

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at hello@markmillmedia.com.
